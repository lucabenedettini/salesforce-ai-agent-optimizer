# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Partner / PRM

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Partner relationship management for channel sales, partner onboarding, deal registration, lead distribution and partner portals.

## Oggetti principali
- Account, Contact, User, Partner Account, Opportunity, Lead.
- AccountContactRelation, ChannelProgram, deal registration objects where enabled.
- Experience Cloud site, roles, permission sets and sharing metadata.

## Funzionalita principali
- Partner onboarding and portal access.
- Deal registration, lead sharing and pipeline collaboration.
- Partner tiers, enablement and reporting.

## Configurazioni principali
- Partner roles, external user profiles, permission sets and sharing rules.
- Experience Cloud pages, navigation, login and self-registration.
- Lead/opportunity assignment, approval and notification flows.
- Reports, dashboards and partner-specific record pages.

## Best practice
- Design account ownership, external roles and sharing before UI.
- Avoid overexposing internal sales data to partners.
- Test partner persona access end to end.
- Keep deal registration criteria and duplicate prevention explicit.

## Contesto versione
Prima della pianificazione leggere `references/salesforce-current-version.md`. Verificare la release della target org e la disponibilita' effettiva delle funzionalita'; per prodotti distribuiti anche come managed package o add-on, verificare la versione installata nella org quando rilevante.
