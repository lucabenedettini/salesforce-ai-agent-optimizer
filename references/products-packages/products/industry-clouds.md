# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Industry Clouds

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Salesforce industry solutions with packaged data models, workflows, UI and compliance patterns for specific industries.

## Oggetti principali
- Industry-specific objects and managed package objects.
- Account, Contact, Case, Opportunity, Product, Asset plus industry extensions.
- OmniStudio assets, data model metadata, permission sets and integration mappings.

## Funzionalita principali
- Prebuilt industry data model and processes.
- Industry-specific UI, automation and analytics.
- Regulatory and operational workflow support.

## Configurazioni principali
- Industry package settings, permission sets, record types and page layouts.
- OmniStudio components, integration procedures and data mappings where used.
- Industry-specific templates, business rules and analytics.
- Data model extension and migration configuration.

## Best practice
- Start from the packaged model and extend only where business differentiation requires it.
- Verify managed-package upgrade constraints before customizing.
- Map standard, industry and custom objects explicitly.
- Test with industry personas, permissions and regulatory scenarios.

## Contesto versione
Prima della pianificazione leggere `references/salesforce-current-version.md`. Verificare la release della target org e la disponibilita' effettiva delle funzionalita'; per prodotti distribuiti anche come managed package o add-on, verificare la versione installata nella org quando rilevante.
