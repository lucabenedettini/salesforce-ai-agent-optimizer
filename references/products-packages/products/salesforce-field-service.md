# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Salesforce Field Service

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Salesforce solution for field operations: work orders, appointments, resources, territories, scheduling, dispatch and mobile execution.

## Oggetti principali
- WorkOrder, WorkOrderLineItem, ServiceAppointment, ServiceResource, AssignedResource.
- ServiceTerritory, ServiceTerritoryMember, WorkType, Skill, SkillRequirement.
- OperatingHours, ResourceAbsence, ServiceCrew, ServiceReport, Asset, ProductItem.

## Funzionalita principali
- Work order and appointment lifecycle.
- Scheduling, dispatch, resource management and skills.
- Field Service mobile app, service reports, inventory and offline work.

## Configurazioni principali
- Field Service settings, work types, service territories, operating hours.
- Service resources, skills, crews, scheduling policies and optimization.
- Mobile settings, service report templates and inventory configuration.
- Permission sets, page layouts, Lightning pages and dispatcher console.

## Best practice
- Model resources, territories, work types and skills before automation.
- Test schedule/dispatch flows with realistic geography and availability.
- Plan mobile/offline behavior for technicians early.
- Validate object access and sharing for dispatchers, technicians and back office.
