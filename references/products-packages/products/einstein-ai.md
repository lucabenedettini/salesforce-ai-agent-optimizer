# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Einstein AI

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Salesforce AI capabilities for predictions, recommendations, generative experiences, scoring and automation across Customer 360.

## Oggetti principali
- Prediction definitions, models, prompts, recommendations, data sources.
- Lead, Opportunity, Case, Knowledge and product-specific AI metadata.
- Permission sets, data access objects, audit and model monitoring records.

## Funzionalita principali
- Predictive scoring and recommendations.
- Generative AI assistance and summaries.
- AI-powered automation and personalization.

## Configurazioni principali
- Model/data setup, prompt templates, grounding data and permissions.
- Einstein feature settings per product cloud.
- Monitoring, feedback, trust and audit configuration.
- Data quality, consent and exclusion rules.

## Best practice
- Verify data quality and bias risk before enabling AI decisions.
- Keep human review for high-impact decisions.
- Limit model access through least privilege.
- Test hallucination, stale data, sensitive data and fallback behavior.
