# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Data 360 / Data Cloud

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Piattaforma dati Salesforce per unificare, armonizzare, risolvere identita, calcolare insight e attivare dati su Customer 360 e Agentforce.

## Oggetti principali
- Data streams, Data Lake Objects, Data Model Objects.
- Identity resolution rulesets, calculated insights, segments, activations.
- Source objects from Sales, Service, Marketing, Commerce and external systems.

## Funzionalita principali
- Data ingestion, mapping and harmonization.
- Identity resolution and unified profiles.
- Calculated insights, segmentation and activation.
- Grounding data for AI and Agentforce.

## Configurazioni principali
- Connectors, data streams, mappings, relationships.
- Identity resolution, match rules, reconciliation rules.
- Calculated insights, segments, activation targets.
- Permission sets, data spaces and governance.

## Best practice
- Definire data owner, freshness, identity keys and consent before activation.
- Avoid copying large data into core CRM when activation or zero-copy patterns fit.
- Validate field mappings, identity rules and activation side effects.
- Plan privacy, retention and access with security stakeholders.
