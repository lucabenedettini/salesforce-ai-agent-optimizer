# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Success Plans / Professional Services

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Salesforce services and support offerings for adoption, governance, implementation guidance, training and operational success.

## Oggetti principali
- Not usually a metadata package; may involve cases, projects, tasks and adoption assets.
- Internal project artifacts, enablement plans, governance records and support cases.

## Funzionalita principali
- Expert guidance, support, health checks, adoption planning and enablement.
- Implementation and architecture assistance.
- Release readiness and operational governance.

## Configurazioni principali
- Success plan entitlements, support contacts and project governance.
- Adoption dashboards, training assets and release calendars.
- Operational runbooks and escalation paths.

## Best practice
- Treat service recommendations as inputs to architecture decisions, not automatic changes.
- Track requirements, owners, acceptance criteria and risks.
- Convert advisory output into concrete metadata/task plans.
- Keep project Knowledge updated with decisions and rationale.

## Contesto versione
Prima della pianificazione leggere `references/salesforce-current-version.md`. Verificare la release della target org e la disponibilita' effettiva delle funzionalita'; per prodotti distribuiti anche come managed package o add-on, verificare la versione installata nella org quando rilevante.
