# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Net Zero Cloud

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Salesforce sustainability solution for emissions accounting, ESG data, supplier data and climate reporting.

## Oggetti principali
- Sustainability account, carbon footprint, energy use, fuel use, emissions factors.
- Supplier, asset, facility, reporting period and disclosure records.
- Managed package objects and analytics assets vary by version.

## Funzionalita principali
- Carbon accounting and emissions calculations.
- ESG data collection, supplier engagement and reporting.
- Sustainability analytics and audit support.

## Configurazioni principali
- Emission factors, calculation methods, reporting periods.
- Data import mappings, source systems and validation rules.
- Permission sets, analytics dashboards and approval flows.
- Industry or regulatory reporting templates.

## Best practice
- Establish auditable data lineage before calculations.
- Lock calculation method changes behind governance.
- Keep units, conversion factors and reporting periods consistent.
- Validate access to sensitive supplier and facility data.
