# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Marketing Cloud

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Famiglia Salesforce per orchestrazione marketing, audience, email, journey, campagne e attivazione dati.

## Oggetti principali
- Campaign, Lead, Contact, Account, Data Extension, Journey, Automation.
- Subscriber, List, Send, Content, Segment, consent/preference records.
- Marketing Cloud Connect mappings where integrated with core Salesforce.

## Funzionalita principali
- Journey orchestration e marketing automation.
- Email, SMS, advertising, personalization e audience activation.
- Sincronizzazione CRM e gestione preferenze/consensi.

## Configurazioni principali
- Business units, sender profiles, data extensions, journeys, automations.
- Marketing Cloud Connect, synchronized data sources, field mappings.
- Consent model, suppression lists, preference center.
- Query activities, segmentation e activation.

## Best practice
- Definire source of truth per consenso, identita e preferenze.
- Verificare limiti, refresh cadence e data retention.
- Non duplicare logica di segmentazione tra CRM, Data 360 e Marketing Cloud senza ownership chiara.
- Testare journey entry/exit criteria e rollback delle audience.
