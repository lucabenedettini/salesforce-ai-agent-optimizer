# Nome del prodotto/pacchetto: Experience Cloud

## Breve descrizione sintetica
Salesforce platform for customer, partner and public digital experiences built on sites, templates, CMS, security and CRM data.

## Oggetti principali
- Network, Site, ExperienceBundle, NavigationMenu, CMS content.
- User, Account, Contact, AccountContactRelation, PermissionSet, Profile.
- Sharing sets, external roles, login/registration metadata, guest user access.

## Funzionalita principali
- Customer and partner portals.
- Authenticated and guest access to CRM data.
- CMS content, self-service, partner collaboration and forms.

## Configurazioni principali
- Site template, theme, pages, navigation, audiences.
- External user profiles, permission sets, sharing sets and roles.
- Login, registration, SSO, guest user access and CSP.
- Lightning components, flows, actions and data visibility.

## Best practice
- Treat guest access as high risk and review every exposed object/field.
- Align external sharing, role hierarchy and permission sets.
- Test pages by persona, device and authentication state.
- Avoid custom UI when standard Experience components meet the need.
